Bio

I am a 37 year-old Texas native, and I styled my first markup in 1999. I've enjoyed watching the web change rapidly into the entirely capable technologies we enjoy today, and am excited to see where we take it next. I've dabbled in PHP, Python, and Ruby on Rails over the last decade, as well as CMS like Wordpress, Magento, and Drupal, but have found the flexibility of building components in frameworks like React and Angular on the front-end to be the work that excites me the most.

While my future is rooted firmly in web technologies, my past has held more than a few wild adventures. I've climbed radio towers for a living, worked on fishing boats based in Dutch Harbor, Alaska and Glouchester, Massachusetts, spent time as a cleanup/recovery worker in New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina, tasked as a Stage Manager for an internationally touring band, owned and operated a bookstore on the San Francisco Bay, backpacked Europe 3 times, Brazil once, and have bartended craft cocktail bars in Fort Worth, Dallas, Oklahoma City, Berkeley, and Napa. With these experiences have come a unique and insightful perspective that enables me to more carefully consider the concerns of customers and anticipate the needs of users around the world.
